OUR MISSION

We believe that voting is a fundamental right, and that felony disenfranchisement laws, which are rooted in a system of structural injustice, infringe upon that right and present yet another barrier to re-entry for those with past felony convictions. OUR TEAM

Free To Vote is comprised of a diverse group of people united around our mission of ending felony disenfranchisement. Our team includes lawyers, community leaders, and social justice advocates, as well as volunteers around the country.
